# Risk & KYC Policy
**Screening:** PEP, sanctions, adverse media daily; re-check on profile updates.

## Levels
- **L0:** email + phone + name (low limits)
- **L1:** government ID + selfie match
- **L2:** source of funds + proof of address

## Re-verification Triggers
- Legal name, DOB, address changes
- Sanctions hit
- Annual refresh for L2

**Note:** For **Gold** users, no repeat KYC if profile unchanged.
